About The Tournament

Supporting Future Legal Professionals

The Colorado Law Classic is an annual charity golf tournament that benefits scholarship funds at the University of Colorado Law School. Join us for a day of golf, networking, and giving back to the legal community.

Event Details

  • Date: Sunday, August 17th, 2025
  • Check-in: 6:30 AM | Start: 7:30 AM
  • Location: City Park Golf Course, Denver, CO
  • Cost: $150 per player | $600 per team (foursome)

Registration Includes

  • 18 holes of golf
  • Lunch
  • Tournament gift
  • Individual and team competition

Frequently Asked Questions

The Colorado Law Classic is a four-person scramble format. Each player tees off, the best shot is selected, and all players play from that spot. This process continues until the ball is holed.

Registration includes 18 holes of golf, cart, lunch, tournament gift, and entry into individual and team competitions.

Yes, City Park Golf Course requires proper golf attire. Collared shirts are required for men, and no denim, cutoffs, or athletic shorts are permitted.

The tournament will proceed rain or shine unless conditions are deemed unsafe by the golf course management. In case of cancellation, a rain date will be announced.

All proceeds from the Colorado Law Classic benefit scholarship funds at the University of Colorado Law School, helping to support the next generation of legal professionals.
