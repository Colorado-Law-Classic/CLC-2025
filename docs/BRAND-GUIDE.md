# Colorado Law Classic Branding Guide

> **Internal Document** — This guide is for internal use by organizers and sponsors.

This guide outlines the visual identity and messaging guidelines for the Colorado Law Classic. Consistency in our branding helps us communicate our mission clearly and strengthens recognition among participants, sponsors and supporters.

---

## Logo Usage

Our logo is a reflection of the spirit of the Colorado Law Classic—an iconic mountain range representing strength and elevation. To maintain its integrity:

- Always maintain the correct aspect ratio. Do not stretch or distort the logo.
- Use the full-color version on light backgrounds. A single-color version may be used on dark backgrounds.
- Allow for ample clear space around the logo. Avoid placing text or graphics too close.

**Logo file:** `assets/img/clc-logo.jpg`

---

## Color Palette

Our brand colors reflect CU Law School's identity: Black and Gold. These colors convey professionalism, prestige, and Colorado pride.

| Color | Hex Code | Usage |
|-------|----------|-------|
| Charcoal | `#1a1a1a` | Primary dark backgrounds, navigation |
| Black | `#0a0a0a` | Deep accents, footer |
| Gold | `#d4af37` | Primary brand accent, buttons, highlights |
| Gold Light | `#e8c860` | Hover states, secondary accents |
| Gold Dark | `#b8962e` | Button hover, active states |
| Cream | `#faf9f7` | Page backgrounds |
| White | `#ffffff` | Card backgrounds, text on dark |

**CSS Variables (defined in `assets/css/styles.css`):**
```css
--clr-black: #0a0a0a;
--clr-charcoal: #1a1a1a;
--clr-gold: #d4af37;
--clr-gold-light: #e8c860;
--clr-gold-dark: #b8962e;
--clr-cream: #faf9f7;
```

## Typography

Our typography pairs elegance with readability.

### Headings: Playfair Display
- Conveys tradition and prestige
- Weights: 600 (semibold), 700 (bold)
- Use for: Page titles, section headers, hero text

### Body: Inter
- Clean, modern readability across all devices
- Weights: 400 (regular), 500 (medium), 600 (semibold), 700 (bold)
- Use for: Body text, navigation, buttons, captions

**Font loading (Google Fonts):**
```html
<link href="https://fonts.googleapis.com/css2?family=Inter:wght@400;500;600;700&family=Playfair+Display:wght@600;700&display=swap" rel="stylesheet">
```

## Imagery & Photography

Photography should capture the energy of the tournament and the camaraderie among participants. Use high-quality images featuring:

- Golfers in action
- Scenic views of the course
- Candid moments of community and celebration
- Trophy presentations and team photos
- Sponsor recognition moments

**Image locations:** `assets/img/` and `assets/img/gallery/`

---

## Tone & Voice

Our communications should be:

- **Warm** — Welcoming to all skill levels and backgrounds
- **Inclusive** — Celebrating the diverse legal community
- **Aspirational** — Inspiring support for future legal professionals
- **Sincere** — Genuine gratitude for sponsors and participants

We celebrate tradition while inspiring the next generation. Speak with clarity and sincerity, using language that conveys excitement about the event and gratitude for our sponsors and participants.

---

## Digital Best Practices

To continue elevating the Colorado Law Classic brand:

1. **Performance** — Optimize images and code for fast page loads
2. **Mobile-friendly** — Ensure responsive design for all screen sizes
3. **Accessibility** — High contrast, alt text, logical heading structure
4. **Impact stories** — Highlight scholarship recipients to connect supporters with the cause
5. **Digital marketing** — Targeted email segments and social media engagement
