Why We Play

The Colorado Law Classic is more than a day on the links—it's an opportunity to support scholarship funds that open doors for future leaders. Your participation directly benefits students at Colorado Law, providing critical financial aid that helps them thrive.

Whether you're teeing off with friends, sponsoring a hole, or making a donation, you are investing in the next generation of legal professionals. Thank you for making a difference.
