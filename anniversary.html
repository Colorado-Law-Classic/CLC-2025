Our Story

For fifteen years, the Colorado Law Classic has brought together alumni, legal professionals, and friends of Colorado Law for a day of friendly competition and meaningful giving.

What started as a small gathering of golf enthusiasts has grown into one of the premier charity events supporting scholarship funds at CU Law School. Each year, participants tee off knowing their entry fees and sponsorships directly support the next generation of legal professionals.

From early morning check-ins to the final putt, the Colorado Law Classic represents the best of our community: camaraderie, generosity, and a shared commitment to education.
