# Colorado Law Classic Website Revamp Recommendations

Based on the analysis of your current website, I've developed comprehensive recommendations for revamping the Colorado Law Classic website. These suggestions aim to enhance visual appeal, improve user experience, and expand functionality while maintaining the essential information and purpose of your charity golf tournament.

## Design Recommendations

### Modern, Professional Design
- **Implement a clean, modern design** with a professional aesthetic that reflects the prestige of both the golf tournament and CU Law School
- **Create a cohesive color scheme** based on CU Law School colors (black and gold) with complementary accents
- **Incorporate golf and legal imagery** throughout the site to reinforce the event's theme
- **Design a hero section** with a high-quality image of City Park Golf Course or previous tournament action
- **Develop custom icons** for key information points (registration, schedule, sponsorship, etc.)

### Responsive and Mobile-Optimized
- **Ensure fully responsive design** that works seamlessly across all devices (desktop, tablet, mobile)
- **Optimize touch targets** for mobile users, especially in registration forms
- **Implement mobile-first navigation** with intuitive menus and clear pathways
- **Create a streamlined mobile registration process** with minimal steps

### Visual Elements
- **Design a refreshed logo** that maintains brand recognition while adding modern touches
- **Create consistent button styles** for all calls-to-action
- **Implement subtle animations** for engagement (hover effects, scroll animations)
- **Use high-quality photography** from previous tournaments
- **Add video content** if available (tournament highlights, testimonials)

## Content Recommendations

### Enhanced Homepage
- **Create an engaging hero section** with tournament date, location, and primary CTA
- **Add a compelling mission statement** about the tournament's purpose and impact
- **Include key statistics** (years running, total funds raised, scholarships funded)
- **Feature testimonials** from past participants and scholarship recipients
- **Highlight sponsors** with logos and recognition
- **Add a countdown timer** to build excitement for the event

### Expanded Information Sections
- **About the Tournament** - History, purpose, impact on CU Law School
- **Event Details** - Schedule, format, prizes, activities, location with map
- **Registration Information** - Player and team options with clear pricing
- **Sponsorship Opportunities** - Detailed benefits for each sponsorship level
- **Photo Gallery** - Images from previous tournaments
- **Impact Stories** - How scholarship funds have helped law students
- **FAQ Section** - Common questions about registration, format, weather policy, etc.
- **Contact Information** - For questions or special arrangements

### Content Strategy
- **Develop storytelling elements** that connect participants to the cause
- **Create content that highlights the dual benefits** of enjoying golf while supporting education
- **Include content about CU Law School** and how scholarships impact students
- **Add testimonials from past participants** about their experience
- **Incorporate content about tournament traditions** or special features

## Functionality Recommendations

### Improved Registration System
- **Streamline the registration process** with fewer steps
- **Create custom registration forms** with clear fields and instructions
- **Implement group registration features** for easy team sign-up
- **Add automated confirmation emails** with event details
- **Create participant accounts** for returning players to store information
- **Implement secure payment processing** with multiple payment options

### Additional Features
- **Interactive course map** with hole information and sponsor recognition
- **Sponsor showcase area** with logos and links to sponsor websites
- **Social media integration** for sharing and promotion
- **Email newsletter signup** for tournament updates
- **Donation option** separate from registration for those who can't attend
- **Weather updates and notifications** as the event approaches
- **Results and recap section** after the event
- **Mobile check-in capability** for tournament day

### Administrative Improvements
- **Custom WordPress dashboard** for easy content updates
- **Registration management system** for tracking participants
- **Reporting tools** for financial tracking and participant statistics
- **Email marketing integration** for communication with registrants
- **SEO optimization** for better visibility

## Technical Recommendations

### Platform Options
1. **WordPress Rebuild (Recommended)**
   - Rebuild on WordPress with a modern theme
   - Utilize WooCommerce for registration and payments
   - Implement custom post types for different content sections
   - Add performance optimization plugins

2. **Static Site with Headless CMS**
   - Create a fast, modern static site with React
   - Implement a headless CMS for easy content management
   - Connect to third-party registration and payment services
   - Optimize for performance and SEO

### Technical Features
- **Fast loading times** through optimization techniques
- **SSL certificate** for security
- **Schema markup** for improved SEO
- **Analytics integration** for tracking site performance
- **Accessibility compliance** for all users
- **Automated backups** for data security
