# Colorado Law Classic Website Analysis

## Current Website Overview

Based on the screenshots provided, the Colorado Law Classic website is a WordPress site that promotes and facilitates registration for an annual charity golf tournament benefiting scholarship funds at CU Law School. The 14th annual event is scheduled for Sunday, August 17th, 2025, at City Park Golf Course.

## Content Analysis

### Key Information Present:
- Event name and branding (Colorado Law Classic)
- Event date (Sunday, August 17th, 2025)
- Purpose (To Benefit Scholarship Funds at CU Law School)
- Location (City Park Golf Course)
- Schedule (Check-in: 6:30 AM, Start: 7:30 AM)
- Player cost ($150 per player)
- What's included (18 holes of golf, lunch, gift, individual and team competition)
- Team registration option (Foursome - $600)
- Sponsorship opportunities (Platinum: $2500, Gold: $2000, Hole Sponsor: $1500, Hole Sponsor without golf: $1000)
- Tax information (approximate value of goods and services exchanged is $100 per golfer)
- Registration options (QR code and website link)

### Navigation and Structure:
- Simple navigation with "Home" and "Register Here" visible in the breadcrumb
- Mobile-responsive design (screenshots appear to be from mobile view)
- Registration page with e-commerce functionality (Add to cart button)
- Logo in header with hamburger menu for navigation

## Design Analysis

### Visual Elements:
- Logo featuring "COLORADO LAW CLASSIC" with mountain graphics
- Clean, simple layout with blue and white color scheme
- Event flyer with Denver skyline background
- Placeholder image on the registration page (missing product image)
- Blue call-to-action buttons ("REGISTER HERE" and "Add to cart")

### User Experience:
- Mobile-friendly design with appropriate text sizing
- Clear call-to-action buttons
- Simple navigation structure
- QR code for easy mobile registration
- E-commerce functionality for processing registrations

## Technical Observations:
- WordPress platform with e-commerce capabilities (likely WooCommerce)
- Mobile-responsive design
- Domain: coloradolawclassic.org
- Registration system with shopping cart functionality

## Areas for Improvement:

### Content and Information:
- Limited information about the tournament's history or impact
- No visible information about past events or testimonials
- Missing details about tournament format, prizes, or activities
- No visible photo gallery from previous events
- Limited information about the beneficiary (CU Law School scholarship funds)

### Design and User Experience:
- Basic design that lacks visual appeal and modern aesthetics
- Missing product image on registration page
- Limited use of visuals and photography
- No apparent social media integration
- Navigation appears minimal with limited sections/pages

### Functionality:
- Registration process could be streamlined
- No visible sponsor recognition area or sponsor logos
- No apparent contact information or FAQ section
- No visible donation option separate from registration/sponsorship
- Limited content about the event beyond basic information

## Strengths to Maintain:
- Clear presentation of essential event information
- Straightforward registration process
- Mobile responsiveness
- Clean, uncluttered layout
- Consistent branding with logo
