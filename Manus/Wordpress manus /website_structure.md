# Colorado Law Classic Website Structure

## Site Map
1. **Home Page**
   - Hero section with event date, location, and registration CTA
   - Brief overview of the tournament and its purpose
   - Key event details (date, time, location, cost)
   - Featured sponsors section
   - Photo highlights from previous events
   - Countdown to event

2. **About Page**
   - Tournament history and purpose
   - Information about beneficiary (CU Law School scholarship funds)
   - Impact of the tournament (funds raised, scholarships awarded)
   - Tournament format and details

3. **Registration Page**
   - Player registration options (individual and team)
   - Pricing information
   - What's included with registration
   - Registration form
   - Payment processing

4. **Sponsorship Page**
   - Sponsorship levels and benefits
   - Pricing for each level
   - Sponsorship registration form
   - Current sponsors section
   - Information on sponsorship impact

5. **Gallery Page**
   - Photos from previous tournaments organized by year
   - Brief descriptions of past events
   - Option to submit photos (for participants)

6. **Past Sponsors Page**
   - Recognition of sponsors from previous years
   - Organized by year and sponsorship level
   - Sponsor logos with links to their websites

7. **FAQ Page**
   - Common questions about registration, format, weather policy
   - Tournament rules and guidelines
   - Contact information for additional questions

8. **Contact Page**
   - Contact form
   - Email and phone information
   - Committee members (if applicable)

## Technical Requirements

1. **Frontend**
   - HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript
   - Responsive design for all devices
   - Optimized images for fast loading
   - Accessibility compliance

2. **Design Elements**
   - Clean, modern aesthetic with subtle golf-themed elements
   - Color scheme: Blues and neutrals with CU Law School color accents
   - Consistent typography throughout
   - Ample white space for readability
   - Subtle animations for enhanced user experience

3. **Functionality**
   - Registration and payment system
   - Photo gallery with filtering by year
   - Sponsor showcase with logo display
   - Contact form with validation
   - Mobile-responsive navigation
   - Social media integration (if needed)

4. **Content Requirements**
   - Tournament logo and branding elements
   - High-quality photos from previous events
   - Sponsor logos (current and past)
   - Event details and schedule
   - Registration information and pricing
   - Sponsorship information and pricing
   - FAQ content
   - Contact information

5. **Development Approach**
   - Static site for optimal performance
   - Simple CMS for easy content updates
   - Deployment to permanent hosting
   - Analytics for visitor tracking
