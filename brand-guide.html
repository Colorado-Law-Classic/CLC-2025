Colorado Law Classic Branding Guide

This guide outlines the visual identity and messaging guidelines for the Colorado Law Classic. Consistency in our branding helps us communicate our mission clearly and strengthens recognition among participants, sponsors and supporters.

Logo Usage

Our logo is a reflection of the spirit of the Colorado Law Classic—an iconic mountain range representing strength and elevation. To maintain its integrity:

  • Always maintain the correct aspect ratio. Do not stretch or distort the logo.
  • Use the full-color version on light backgrounds. A single-color version may be used on dark backgrounds.
  • Allow for ample clear space around the logo. Avoid placing text or graphics too close.

Tone & Voice

Our communications should be warm, inclusive and aspirational. We celebrate tradition while inspiring the next generation. Speak with clarity and sincerity, using language that conveys excitement about the event and gratitude for our sponsors and participants.
