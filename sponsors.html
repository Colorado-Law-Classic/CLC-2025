Become a Sponsor

Your partnership helps fund scholarships and makes this event special. Sponsors enjoy brand visibility, complimentary player packages and unique networking opportunities.

Sponsorship Levels:

  • Platinum – $2,500: Includes golf for four players, signage on the course and at registration, and recognition in all event materials.
  • Gold – $2,000: Includes golf for four players, signage at one hole and name recognition in event materials.
  • Hole Sponsor – $1,500: Includes golf for two players and signage at one hole.
  • Hole Sponsor (without golf) – $1,000: Signage at one hole and recognition in event materials.
