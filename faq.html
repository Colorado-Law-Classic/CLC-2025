Frequently Asked Questions

If you have questions about the tournament, we have answers! If you don’t see your question here, please email us at coloradolawgolf@gmail.com.

What should I wear?

Golf-appropriate attire is required. Please wear collared shirts and golf shoes. Denim of any kind is not permitted. Dress in layers for September weather in Denver and don’t forget sunscreen and a hat!

Where do I park?

Parking is available on-site at City Park Golf Course. Volunteers will direct you to the reserved tournament parking area on the day of the event.

Is my registration tax-deductible?

For income tax purposes, the approximate value of goods and services exchanged per golfer is $100. Please consult your tax advisor regarding tax-deductible amounts.

What’s the event schedule?

Check-in opens at 7:00 AM and the first tee time is at 8:00 AM. Breakfast will be available during check-in. Lunch and awards take place immediately following play.

Can I register as an individual?

Absolutely! Individual players are welcome. We will pair you with other individual registrants to form a team.

How do sponsors benefit?

Sponsors receive brand exposure on our website and at the event, recognition on social media, complimentary golfer packages and opportunities to network with Colorado Law alumni and the local legal community. Custom sponsorship packages are available.

Is there a rain date?

The tournament will be played rain or shine. In the event of severe weather, we will coordinate with the course and notify participants of alternate arrangements.

How can I volunteer?

We couldn’t run the event without volunteers! Please email us at coloradolawgolf@gmail.com if you’re interested in donating your time.
